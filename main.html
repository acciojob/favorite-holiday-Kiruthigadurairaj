Abu Dhabi, UAE


Abu Dhabi is the capital city of the United Arab Emirates.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque: The largest mosque in the UAE
  2. Louvre Abu Dhabi: A museum with works from various ages and cultures, housed in a 7,500 ton dome with eight layers
  3. Ferrari World: The first Ferrari-branded theme park, with rides, shows, shopping, and dining
  4. Yas Waterworld: An Emirati-themed water park with over 40 rides, including the Bandit Bomber, the longest suspended roller coaster in the Middle East
  5. Yas Marina Circuit: A car racing track that hosts the Formula 1 Etihad Airways Abu Dhabi Grand Prix annually

Must-try Foods

  • MajboosA traditional rice dish made with whole spices, dried lemon, and locally caught shrimp, lamb, or chicken.
  • ShawarmaA non-vegetarian dish of chicken, beef, or lamb slices rolled in flatbread or roti and filled with veggies, sauce, and onions.
  • HareesA traditional dish made with minced meat, crushed wheat, salt, onions, and other ingredients.
  • BalaleetA sweet and salty breakfast dish made with vermicelli, rose water, cinnamon, saffron, and an egg omelette on top.
  • MadroubaA traditional Emirati dish made by mashing fish with spices to create a thick, savory texture.
